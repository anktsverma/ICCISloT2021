About Conference

The proposed 3rd International Conference on Computational Intelligence,Security & Internet of Thing (ICCISIoT),2020 will be held at National Institute of Technology,Agartala, India to enable researchers buil connections between different digital technologies based on Computational Intelligence,Security an Internet of Things (IoT).

Through sharing and networking, ICCISIoT 2020 will provide an opportunity for researchers, practitioners and educators to exchange research evidence, practical experiences and innovative ideas on issues relate to the Conference theme.

Prospective authors are encouraged to contribute to and help shape the conference through submission o their research papers.Also high quality researh contributions describing original and unpublished results of conceptual, constructive, empirical, experimentalot theorical work in all areas of Computational Intelligence Security and Internet of Things (IoT) arecordially invited for presentation at the conference.

The conference solicits contributionsof papers that address themes and topics of the conference, including figures,tables and references of novel research materials.

Key Note Speaker

Prof. K. M. Deb

Professor & Koenig Endowed Chair, Electrical and Computer Engineering, Michigan State University, USA

Kalyanmoy Deb is Koenig Endowed Chair Professor at Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering in Michigan State University, USA. Born in Udaipur, Tripura, he passed his higher secondary school from Netaji Subhas Vidyaniketan in 1980 and went on to receive his BTech degree from IIT Kharagpur. Prof. Deb's research interests are in evolutionary optimization and their application in multi-criterion optimization, modeling, and machine learning. He was awarded IEEE Evolutionary Computation Pioneer Award for his sustained work in EMO, Infosys Prize, TWAS Prize in Engineering Sciences, CajAstur Mamdani Prize, Distinguished Alumni Award from IIT Kharagpur, Edgeworth-Pareto award, Bhatnagar Prize in Engineering Sciences, and Bessel Research award from Germany. He is fellow of IEEE, ASME, and three Indian science and engineering academies. He has published over 545 research papers with Google Scholar citation of over 145,000 with h-index 121. He is in the editorial board on 18 major international journals. More information about his research contribution can be found from http://www.egr.msu.edu/~kdeb.

Publishing and Indexing

This year ICCISIoT 2020 adopted pre-conference publication policy.So the online proceedings will be available during conference dates.All accepted and registered papers will be published as one volume in the Communication in Computer and Information Science (CCIS) series by Springer, and also are also indexed by SCOPUS, DBLP, EI- Compendex, Thomson Reuters Conference Proceedings Citation (Former ISI Proceedings)- an integrated index within Web of Science and Springerlink.
